About The Run

More Than Just a Run

The Run for Literacy was an incredible experience! The energy was electric, and knowing we were running for such an important cause made every step worthwhile.

Organized by the students of R.A. Podar College, this event combines fitness with financial education, creating a unique platform to raise awareness about the importance of financial literacy.
